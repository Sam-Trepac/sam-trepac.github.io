G201 -- Be a good person.
Description Follow the FIRST Code of Conduct.
Penalty Verbal Warning Yellow Card if Subsequent during event
G202 -- DRIVE TEAM interactions.
Description A human may only enter the FIELD during pre-MATCH to setup their ROBOT and after a MATCH is over to stop and collect their ROBOT after being instructed to do so by the Head REFEREE (or designee).
Penalty Verbal Warning Yellow Card if Subsequent during event
G203 -- Asking other teams to throw a MATCH – not cool.
Description A team may not encourage an ALLIANCE of which it is not a member to play beneath its ability.
Penalty Verbal Warning Red Card if Subsequent during event
G204 -- Letting someone coerce you into throwing a MATCH.
Description A team, as the result of encouragement by a team not on their ALLIANCE, may not play beneath its ability.
Penalty Verbal Warning Red Card if subsequent during event
G205 -- Throwing your own MATCH is bad.
Description A team may not intentionally lose a MATCH or sacrifice RANKING POINTS to lower their own ranking or manipulate another team’s ranking.
Penalty Verbal Warning Red Card if subsequent during event
G206 -- Don't violate rules for RPs.
Description Teams or ALLIANCES may not collude to intentionally violate a rule in an attempt to influence RANKING POINTS.
Penalty Yellow Card ALLIANCE ineligible for PATTERN and GOAL RPs
G207 -- Do not abuse ARENA access.
Description Non-DRIVE TEAM members with special access (e.g., media badges) may not assist, coach, or signal during a MATCH. Safety exceptions only.
Penalty Verbal Warning Yellow Card if subsequent during event
G208 -- Show up to your MATCHES.
Description If a ROBOT has passed inspection, at least one DRIVE TEAM member must report to the ARENA and participate in each assigned Qualification MATCH.
Penalty Disqualified from the current MATCH
G209 -- Keep your ROBOT together.
Description A ROBOT may not intentionally detach or leave any part on the FIELD.
Penalty Red Card
G210 -- Do not expect to gain by doing others harm.
Description Actions aimed at forcing the opponent ALLIANCE to violate a rule are not allowed. The targeted ALLIANCE will not receive a penalty.
Penalty Minor Foul Major Foul if repeated No penalty assigned to targeted ALLIANCE
G211 -- Egregious or exceptional violations.
Description Egregious behavior or repeated violations of any rule or procedure may result in a CARD at any time during the event, assigned by the Head REFEREE.
Penalty Yellow Card Red Card (if severe)
G212 -- All teams can play.
Description A team may not encourage another team to exclude their ROBOT or get DISQUALIFIED from a Qualification MATCH for any reason.
Penalty Yellow Card Red Card if the ROBOT does not participate
