G428 -- No wandering.
Description DRIVE TEAM members must remain in their designated ALLIANCE AREA.
  • A. DRIVE TEAMS may be anywhere in their respective ALLIANCE AREA during a MATCH.
  • B. DRIVE TEAM members must be staged inside their respective ALLIANCE AREA prior to MATCH start.
Penalty Verbal Warning Minor Foul if subsequent violations occur during the event
Notes DRIVE TEAM members may retrieve SCORING ELEMENTS that have left the FIELD if they can do so without violating G428, G430, or G434. Reintroduction must follow G433.
Exceptions are granted for safety or actions that are inadvertent, MOMENTARY, and inconsequential.
G429 -- DRIVE COACHES and other teams: hands off the controls.
Description A ROBOT shall be operated only by the DRIVERS of that team. DRIVE COACHES may not handle gamepads.

DRIVE COACHES may assist the DRIVERS only in the following ways:
  • A. Holding the DRIVER STATION device
  • B. Troubleshooting the DRIVER STATION device
  • C. Selecting OpModes on the DRIVER STATION app
  • D. Pressing the INIT button on the DRIVER STATION app
  • E. Pressing the (▶) start button on the DRIVER STATION app
  • F. Pressing the (■) stop button on the DRIVER STATION app
Penalty Major Foul Yellow Card if greater-than-momentary
G430 -- DRIVE COACHES, SCORING ELEMENTS are off limits.
Description DRIVE COACHES may not contact SCORING ELEMENTS, unless for safety purposes.
Penalty Minor Foul
G431 -- DRIVE TEAMS, watch your reach.
Description Once a MATCH starts, a DRIVE TEAM member inside the FIELD may not:
  • A. Directly contact a ROBOT.
  • B. Contact a SCORING ELEMENT in contact with a ROBOT.
  • C. Disrupt SCORING ELEMENT scoring.
  • D. Contact a FIELD element.
Penalty Major Foul + Yellow Card if G431.A Red Card + Opposing ALLIANCE awarded PATTERN RP if G431.C
Notes Exceptions are granted for safety or actions that are inadvertent, MOMENTARY, and inconsequential.
For G431.A, the penalty applies regardless of whether the DRIVE TEAM member or ROBOT initiates contact.

Impacting ARTIFACT scoring includes:
  • A. Contacting an ARTIFACT LAUNCHED by the opponent within the FIELD.
  • B. Contacting an ARTIFACT in the opponent’s GOAL.
  • C. Disrupting the scoring of an ARTIFACT on the opponent’s RAMP or by operating the opponent’s GATE.
  • D. A DRIVE TEAM member LAUNCHING an ARTIFACT into a GOAL.
G432 -- Humans, only meddle with ARTIFACTS in the LOADING ZONE.
Description DRIVE TEAM members may only introduce ARTIFACTS to, remove ARTIFACTS from, or move ARTIFACTS within the LOADING ZONE and only the LOADING ZONE. Actions must occur:
  • A. only during TELEOP,
  • B. without using a tool,
  • C. without causing an ARTIFACT to enter into the LOADING ZONE from elsewhere on the FIELD, and
  • D. without causing an ARTIFACT to leave the LOADING ZONE and enter the rest of the FIELD unless the ARTIFACT is CONTROLLED by a ROBOT as follows:
    • I. ARTIFACT CONTROL begins when the ROBOT is in the LOADING ZONE, and
    • II. ARTIFACT is still CONTROLLED by the ROBOT when the ROBOT leaves the LOADING ZONE.
Penalty Minor Foul per ARTIFACT Major Foul per ARTIFACT that enters top of GOAL
G433 -- Humans may only enter SCORING ELEMENTS.
Description DRIVE TEAM members may only enter ARTIFACTS onto the FIELD
Penalty Minor Foul per non-ARTIFACT item entered onto the FIELD.
G434 -- The ALLIANCE AREA has a storage limit.
Description During TELEOP, each ALLIANCE may not store more than 6 ARTIFACTS out of play. DRIVE TEAM members making a good-faith effort to immediately re-enter additional ARTIFACTS into play are exempt.
Penalty Minor Foul per ARTIFACT over the limit Additional Minor Foul per ARTIFACT over limit every 3 seconds until corrected
