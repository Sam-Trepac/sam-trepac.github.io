G409 -- ROBOTS must be under control.
Description A ROBOT must not pose an undue hazard to humans or ARENA elements. This includes disrupting anything or contacting a person outside the FIELD, or operating in a dangerous manner.
Penalty Disabled Verbal Warning Yellow Card if repeated or subsequent during event
Notes
  • Be aware of REFEREES and FIELD STAFF near the ARENA.
  • Examples: flailing outside the FIELD, knocking over a DRIVER STATION stand, moving/damaging timer display, or contacting FIELD STAFF.
  • Contact with elements outside the FIELD perimeter (e.g., DRIVER STATION stand or floor) is not a violation.
G410 -- ROBOTS must stop when instructed.
Description If instructed to DISABLE by a REFEREE, a DRIVE TEAM member must immediately press the stop (■) button on the DRIVER STATION app.
Penalty Major Foul for greater-than-momentary delay Red Card if continuous
G411 -- ROBOTS must be identifiable.
Description A ROBOT’s team number and ALLIANCE color must remain clear and identifiable at all times, as determined by the Head REFEREE.
Penalty Verbal Warning Minor Foul if subsequent during event
G412 -- Don’t damage the FIELD.
Description ROBOTS may not damage FIELD elements during play.
Penalty Verbal Warning Disabled if further damage likely Yellow Card for any subsequent damage during event
Notes Corrective action (e.g., removing or modifying damaging mechanisms) and reinspection may be required before competing again. G412 does not apply to damage caused by normal gameplay actions.
G413 -- Watch your ARENA interaction.
Description ROBOTS may not grab, grasp, attach to, entangle with, or suspend from ARENA elements, except for permitted SCORING ELEMENT interactions (see G407).
Penalty Major Foul Yellow Card if repeated or greater-than-momentary Disabled if further damage likely
Notes
  • Corrective action (removal or modification of mechanism, reinspection) may be required.
  • ROBOTS operating the GATE should push the lever down to open; pulling or applying closing force violates this rule.
G414 -- ROBOTS have horizontal expansion limits.
Description ROBOTS must comply with horizontal expansion limits per R105.A during the MATCH. Over-expansion due to damage is permitted if not used strategically.
Penalty Minor Foul Major Foul if used for strategic benefit or affects scoring
G415 -- ROBOTS have vertical expansion limits (with exceptions).
Description ROBOTS must comply with vertical expansion limits per R105. Expansion above 18 in. (45.7 cm) up to 38 in. (96.5 cm) is allowed only during the final 20 seconds of the MATCH and when not in any LAUNCH ZONE.
Penalty Minor Foul Major Foul if used for strategic benefit or affects scoring
G416 -- LAUNCHING in the LAUNCH ZONE only.
Description ROBOTS may only LAUNCH SCORING ELEMENTS when inside a LAUNCH ZONE or overlapping a LAUNCH LINE.
Penalty Minor Foul per launched SCORING ELEMENT Major Foul per launched SCORING ELEMENT if it enters open top of GOAL
Notes
  • “LAUNCHING” includes shooting, propelling, or forcefully throwing SCORING ELEMENTS.
  • “Bulldozing” or short-distance expulsion during normal operation is not LAUNCHING.
  • Intent is not to penalize manipulators expelling elements through standard play (e.g., intake reversal).
