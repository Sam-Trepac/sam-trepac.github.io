G403 -- ROBOTS are motionless between AUTO and TELEOP.
Description Any powered movement of the ROBOT or its MECHANISMS is not allowed during the transition period between AUTO and TELEOP.
Penalty Major Foul
Notes
  • Movement caused by inertia, gravity, or actuator de-energizing is not a violation.
  • Teams may stop AUTO or initialize TELEOP during the transition, but INIT must not cause motion. If it does, wait until TELEOP starts.
  • Strategic violations (e.g., launching elements, operating the gate, or significant robot motion) are considered egregious behavior under G211.
G404 -- ROBOTS are motionless at the end of TELEOP.
Description ROBOTS must stop all powered movement after TELEOP ends until the Head REFEREE or designee signals retrieval is allowed.
Penalty Minor Foul Major Foul per ARTIFACT launched into GOAL after TELEOP Major Foul if ROBOT contacts GATE after TELEOP
Notes
  • Movement from inertia, gravity, or actuator de-energizing is not powered movement.
